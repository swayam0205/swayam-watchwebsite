About Timora

Timora – A Timeless Collection is a luxury watch portfolio crafted to reflect elegance, precision, and heritage. Our curated collection showcases a diverse range of timepieces — from modern chronographs to vintage classics — each representing a perfect blend of craftsmanship and innovation.

Whether you're a collector, enthusiast, or simply someone who appreciates fine design, Timora aims to deliver a digital experience as timeless as the watches it presents.

This website is developed as part of an internship project, with special focus on backend development using Flask, dynamic routing, and UI collaboration.
